About Me

The Prickly King

I'm Spike, the undisputed king of the desert, a master of cacti and a force to be reckoned with.
My precise shots, fired from my cactus-like form, pierce through the air with deadly accuracy, leaving enemies in their wake. When the battlefield demands it, I unleash my Super, transforming it into a prickly minefield, slowing and damaging any unfortunate souls who dare to trespass.
Whether battling solo or alongside my teammates, I prove to be an invaluable asset, always ready to adapt and overcome any challenge.
